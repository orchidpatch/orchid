Orchid's Hypertext Curiosity

Heron, Crocodile; Crocodile, Heron

The Blue Heron cricks-cracks
Crocodile. No more growth,
in one sense. In another?

The inherent erogeny of
the swallow, the digestion,
the interchange of selves...

the swallow, this sin, clasped
they enlarge Earth, Blue Sky;
Blue Heron, you and I.

November 2020
